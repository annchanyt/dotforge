Dotforge Health

Dotforge health is focused on the opportunity for data powered healthcare innovation.

By enabling people to live healthier lifestyles and engage with their healthcare providers, digital technology has the potential to relieve a great deal of the pressure on clinicians, hospitals and the healthcare system at large. Whether software or hardware, solutions will produce a significant amount of useful data resulting in an increasingly valuable resource for improving future patient care and efficiency.

Our aim is to fast track the development of startups that address specific needs in the market. Our partnerships with leading data experts, data providers and users, allow our teams to benefit from a wealth of industry insight as they build their companies right next to one of the world’s largest health care resources – the HS CIC. Dotforge Health will integrate your startup into the healthcare ecosystem ensuring the products being developed are in tune with the constraints and needs of this market.

Our 13 week programme provides health-tech entrepreneurs with the support to build, position and launch a product that addresses a tangible need within the healthcare sector. We offer co-working space, funding and an extensive network of mentors to aid in the growth and development of cutting edge health-tech startups.

“We invest in tech startups or teams building products that could have a social impact.”
