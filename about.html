About Dotforge

Dotforge is a pre-seed startup accelerator that supports talented entrepreneurs to build visionary companies.

Driven by the belief that the key to a better tomorrow exists in digital innovation, the programme was established in 2012 by a community of entrepreneurs committed to connecting startups with the business and investor networks in the UK.

We provide teams with an initial investment, 13 weeks of intensive mentoring, and access to a global network of business leaders and investors to help companies reach their growth potential.

Our goal is to create an inclusive start-up community that leverages collective experience and networks to create successful companies based in the North of England.

Dotforge is now developing sector-focused programmes to build on the strengths of key cities in the North and to create a global conversation on innovation and change.

“We invest in tech startups or teams building products that could have a social impact.”
