Our People

We consider Dotforge to be an ever-growing family of founders, companies big and small in the UK, and of domains various, helping our new companies and teams to build a product that people will love. That’s a lot of people not listed on this page, making this happen. But here you will find the key personalities driving this forward. You can also find a list of mentors that support our mission and that you can connect with through us. But above all, we think that it is our family of founders and senior associate companies, who have been themselves through the similar journey of taking an idea from shipped product, and thereupon to market, that are the most valuable help for our new early stage founders.

Executive Team

Emma Cheshire
Managing Director

CEO and co-founder of Dotforge, Emma raised the investment funding of £500,000 for the first Dotforge programmes which she went on to develop and deliver in 2013 and 2014. She is now building a wider portfolio of programmes for the north of England, which has included securing £1.5million investment in partnership with Key Fund and the RSA to pilot Dotforge Impact, a Social Accelerator in Sheffield. Emma is an industry representative on the Tech Cluster Alliance for TechCity UK and has a background in tech innovation and integration for the Third Sector, as well as fund management.

Tobias StoneVenture Partner

An entrepreneur having worked in the technology and finance sectors for many years, Tobias brings together a background in innovation policy, economic development, investment, and entrepreneurship. He is currently running EyeFocus Accelerator, a unique specialist digital health accelerator that focusses on creating value through building the a sector-specific innovation ecosystem around an accelerator program. He is closely with Bayer Health Care, Carl Zeiss, Bosch, and leading global hospitals, universities, and investors in health care innovation. The accelerator is a commercial venture with a strong social undertone, supporting both early stage startups and developed businesses in the vertical. Tobias is also a Collaborative Research Fellow at the University of Huddersfield, and an active advocate of the Northern tech ecosystem.
